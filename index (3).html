About Us

Silicon Valley Stays was made to provide affordable and lively accomodations for those visiting silicon valley. We wanted to provide lodging for the vast majority of tourists who wanted to explore everything Silicon Valley has to offer. Our rooms and services are well kept and we belive in the customer satisfaction in everything we do. We pride ourselves for having the best location possible for tourists and travelers, San Jose.

Ammenities And Services

Here at Silicon Valley Stays, we are proud of having a ton of servcices for an affordable price.

Breakfast Options

We have a variety of different breakfast items that alternate all of the time. All of the options are at a low price and are conviently located in the hotel. Basic breakfast is not included, but there is room service for selected rooms.

Room service

From our upgraded menu, we offer room service to certain rooms. One can order food or any other service at all time except before 7:00 am and after 11:00 pm. Any requests made before or after these times will not get tended to.

Our room service covers all items displayed on the menu. Room service staff will be able to assist you 10-15 minutes after requested.

To see more, visit the Updates tab!

Pool

Yes, we have a pool! Our pool is well maintianed and avaliable 24/7. There is also a hottub on the far side of the pool.
